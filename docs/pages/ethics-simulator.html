Ethics Engine

Ethical Dilemma Simulator

Navigate complex logistics decisions with AI-generated scenarios, Monte Carlo simulation, and multi-framework ethical evaluation.

AI-Generated Ethical Scenarios

Using Google Gemini, we generate realistic ethical dilemmas that logistics managers face daily. Each scenario presents a difficult choice with competing stakeholder interests.

  • Realistic Dilemmas - Based on actual industry challenges
  • Multiple Stakeholders - Drivers, customers, company, community
  • Monte Carlo Simulation - Analyze outcome probabilities

Multi-Framework Evaluation

Utilitarian Ethics

Greatest good for the greatest number. Evaluates total impact across all stakeholders.

Deontological Ethics

Duty-based reasoning. Evaluates adherence to moral rules and obligations.

Virtue Ethics

Character-based approach. Evaluates what a virtuous person would do.
