Intelligent Risk Assessment

Proactively identify and mitigate operational risks with ML-powered multi-factor analysis and SHAP explainability.

Multi-Factor Risk Scoring

Our risk assessment engine combines multiple data sources to compute comprehensive risk scores for each vehicle in your fleet.

  • Weather Conditions - Rain, fog, wind, temperature extremes
  • Traffic Patterns - Congestion, accidents, construction zones
  • Driver Fatigue - Hours driven, break compliance, historical data
  • Vehicle Health - Maintenance schedules, sensor alerts, diagnostics

SHAP Explainability

Understand exactly why a risk score was assigned with SHAP (SHapley Additive exPlanations) values that break down each contributing factor.

  • Transparent Decisions - No black-box predictions
  • Feature Attribution - See which factors matter most
  • Actionable Insights - Know what to fix to reduce risk
