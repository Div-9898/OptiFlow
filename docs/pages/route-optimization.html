Our VRP Arena uses Google OR-Tools to solve complex routing problems with multiple vehicles, capacity constraints, time windows, and driver shift limits.

Optimization Strategies

Path Cheapest Arc

Fast heuristic that builds routes by always selecting the cheapest next destination.

Savings Algorithm

Classic Clarke-Wright algorithm that merges routes to maximize distance savings.

Guided Local Search

Metaheuristic that escapes local optima through penalty-guided exploration.
